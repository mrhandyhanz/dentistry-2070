01. Institutional Mandate

Protecting the Integrity of Long-term Forecasting.

Our mandate is to provide a 44-year forecasting horizon that prioritizes global patient outcomes over quarterly profit cycles. To achieve this, all research outputs are owned by the GMKAI Global Endowment, a non-distributing legal entity.

No corporate partner, regardless of tier, may influence the parameters of a forecasting model or suppress findings that suggest a disruption to current dental industry business models.
