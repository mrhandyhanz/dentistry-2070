About the Institute

Why 2070?

In 2070, a child born today will be entering middle age. The decisions we make now about oral health research, education, and policy will shape their experience of dental care throughout their lives.

Dentistry 2070 was founded on a simple premise: the most significant transformations in oral health care will unfold over decades, not years. To understand and responsibly shape these changes, we need institutions designed for long-term thinking.

Generational Perspective

Our 44-year research horizon allows us to study transformations that unfold across generations.

Global Scope

Oral health inequalities are a global challenge. Our research addresses both developed and developing contexts.
